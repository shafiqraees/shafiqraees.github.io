GO CARBON NEUTRAL

Based on the idea of by Professor in Hawaii, USA the aim of this project is to reduce Carbon emission by planting trees. People signs up in this web application and enlisted in seperate teams On specific day each team is alloted a site where they plant trees. These team mostly contain students and teachers from local elementry schools

Per tree planting a ticket is issued to each player and the team which plants most trees get their team picture on main page of site
